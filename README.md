# mtg-sell-signal · daily "has it peaked?" forecast

Dated, falsifiable forecasts of Magic: The Gathering cards that look to have **peaked** - likely to
stall and/or fall over the next **3 / 6 / 12 months**, so you might consider selling. Generated by a
calibrated machine-learning model (the mirror image of a price-spike predictor), published every
morning. By **Cameraderie Cards** · cameraderiecards.com

> Part of the **Cameraderie Cards** toolkit: [`mtg-buy-signal`](https://github.com/rnorlund/mtg-buy-signal) · [`mtg-sell-signal`](https://github.com/rnorlund/mtg-sell-signal) (you are here) · [`mtg-reprint-signal`](https://github.com/rnorlund/mtg-reprint-signal)

> Informational only. Not financial advice. Probabilities are estimates over a fixed historical
> regime; trust the *ranking* of picks more than the raw probability.
